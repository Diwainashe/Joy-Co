# Joy&Co Party Rentals - Project Context

**Company:** Joy&Co (South Africa-based)
**Design Chosen:** Design 2 - Vibrant & Bold 🎉
**Currency:** ZAR (South African Rand)
**Target Market:** Party planners, event organizers in South Africa
**Platform Type:** B2C party/event item rental e-commerce + admin inventory management

## Key Requirements
- Customer-facing: Browse rental items via colorful tile cards
- Admin: Upload inventory items, bundle into packages, manage inventory
- SEO & AI optimized for search visibility
- Professional yet cutting-edge aesthetic
- Multi-color vibrant design system

## Localization Details
- Currency: ZAR (R symbol, format: R45.00)
- Region: South Africa (consider major cities: Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria)
- Payment methods: EFT, Credit Card, Possibly Yoco/PayFast for SA
- Delivery/logistics: Local to South African delivery areas
- Time zone: SAST (South African Standard Time, UTC+2)
- Language: English (primary), possibly Afrikaans support future

## Design System
- Color Palette: 8+ vibrant colors (blue, green, pink, purple, amber, coral, yellow, orange)
- Typography: Poppins + Open Sans
- Style: Bento grid hero, color-rotated product cards, vibrant CTAs
- Admin: Light mode with colorful stat cards
